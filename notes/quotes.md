# Quotes That Actually Mean Something

No inspirational poster garbage. These are quotes that changed how I think.

---

## On Action

> "The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now."
> — Chinese proverb

Stop waiting for perfect conditions. They don't exist.

---

## On Understanding

> "If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough."
> — Often attributed to Einstein (probably not his)

Complexity is often hiding incomplete understanding.

---

## On Life

> "We suffer more often in imagination than in reality."
> — Seneca

95% of what I worry about never happens. The other 5% isn't as bad as I imagined.

---

## On Travel

> "Travel is fatal to prejudice, bigotry, and narrow-mindedness."
> — Mark Twain

Impossible to hate people after eating their grandmother's cooking.

---

## On Physics

> "The universe is under no obligation to make sense to you."
> — Neil deGrasse Tyson

Nature doesn't care about our intuition. Quantum mechanics proved that.

---

## On Focus

> "You can do anything, but not everything."
> — David Allen

Said no to 100 opportunities this year. Said yes to 3. Best decision ever.

---

## On Time

> "Time is what we want most, but what we use worst."
> — William Penn

We act like time is infinite. We act like people are immortal. Both are false.

---

## On Learning

> "I know that I know nothing."
> — Socrates

The more physics I learn, the more I realize how little I understand. That's the point.

---

## On Perspective

> "We're just a rock, orbiting a mediocre star, in an unremarkable galaxy, in an indifferent universe."
> — Paraphrased from Carl Sagan

Weirdly comforting. Your embarrassing moment? The universe doesn't care.

---

## On Simplicity

> "Perfection is achieved not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away."
> — Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

Best code I've written is code I deleted.

---

## On Now

> "Yesterday is history, tomorrow is a mystery, but today is a gift. That's why it's called the present."
> — Master Oogway (yes, from Kung Fu Panda)

Animated turtle spitting facts.
